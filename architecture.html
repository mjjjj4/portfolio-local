Project 01

The Modern Residence

A study in understated luxury — this residential project balances warm natural materials with crisp architectural lines. The brief called for spaces that feel expansive yet intimate, with light as the primary design element. Every render was composed to capture a specific time of day and emotional temperature.

Project 02

Urban Loft Series

High-rise living reimagined for a generation that works and plays at home. This series explores raw concrete juxtaposed with soft textiles and ambient light — a visual narrative of urban energy brought indoors. Each frame tells its own story while remaining part of a cohesive series.

Project 03

The Pavilion Concept

A speculative design exploring how public and private space can dissolve into each other through material lightness and strategic transparency. This conceptual pavilion was visualised across multiple lighting scenarios — from harsh midday sun to golden hour — each revealing a different facet of the structure's relationship with its surroundings.
